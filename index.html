Joy Bathrooms business began in 1980 at its first site near Ectovile in Nairobi. However the current owners took over in the year 2001. Today, Joy Bathrooms is a private company and among Kenya’s most successful manufacturing and supplier of plumbing and bathroom products.

  • For over 30 years Joy Bathrooms has provided products and a dedicated service to the construction industry.
  • We’re proud of the reputation we have earned over that time for “having what you need, when you need it, where you need it.”
  • As the plumbing industry has become more specialized, Joy Bathrooms has expanded into the allied areas of solar water heating as well as instant water heaters which services commercial plumbers and volume home builders.
  • Our technically qualified personnel gives free technical advise on products and installations. We also stock spares for our products.

Joy bathrooms currently has one showroom where retail customers can enjoy a huge range of locally manufactured bathroom products including Kenyan and international brands. Our showroom also displays a range of kitchen products.

Why Choose Joy Bathroom?

Joy Bathrooms has pioneered many of the services and initiatives in the industries in which we operate. We are honored to be the only manufacturer of acrylic bathtubs in the East and Central Africa region. Joy Bathrooms has also influenced trends in bathroom products and design in Kenya.
